FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Of course, If you don't see an answer to your question, don't hesitate to contact me.

I offer both general and specialized language lessons in English, Czech and French.

You can also contact me for tutoring, graduation preparation, preparation for language state exams and job interviews in those languages.

Apart from that, I offer content editing, proofreading, translation, essay writing, and presentation preparation.

Most of my students are looking for individual, fully custom approach, where the focus is uniquely on them and their goals and they can cast away their shyness.

They want to feel comfortable and relaxed while communicating in their desired language.

They're motivated by the idea of being able to speak without fear, making less mistakes and being able to lead a conversation in a foreign language, whether that makes them the spokesperson for their family on vacation or empowers them to deal with foreign-language aspects of their job on a professional level.

Most students can notice their progress really fast, generally. Especially where it matters most – in spoken interaction.

They feel way more confident thanks to their new skills and they want to put them to use!

I’m a perky, light-hearted person, so I like to bring loads of energy and humor to each lesson.

I come up with exciting, creative and effective activities, tools, and materials to fit the student‘s needs at the exact moment.

I use empathy to gauge how much energy a student has and what mood they‘re in and am ready to adjust the flow of the lesson to it.

My students love that I'm always in a good mood. Nothing escapes me, though ☺

Based on your language level, needs and goals, we'll focus on a unique combination of the following language areas:

  1. Introduction to the language - always within the context of your native language
  2. Basic phrases for your purposes
  3. Genuine conversation giving context to your vocabulary expansion
  4. A firm, but relaxed grasp of grammar rules
  5. Pronunciation and foreign accent reduction

I design my lessons on the basis of my original methodology.

I put a lot of emphasis on continuity that carries the context of what we discuss or just touch upon through our individual lessons.

It helps the student retain the majority of the learning content.

My students always get to immerse in the world that the new language opens up to them.

Together, we soak up the culture of the country, along with the nature of its people, the way they communicate, the manneurisms and traditions they cherish.

We cover all the areas that make the natives who they are.

This way, my students can truly become a part of the community when speaking the language.

My lessons are intended for one to three students.

Some prefer a one-on-one approach that focuses uniquely on them whereas others take my lessons with a friend or a family member that has a similar language level.

This adds healthy competition and mutual feedback to the mix and makes many students even more motivated.
