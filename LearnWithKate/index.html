You Don't Have to Struggle

Learning any language by yourself can be a huge challenge.

You need to learn so many little things to become a skilled communicator in your chosen language.

Having a teacher, a guide, a helping hand who knows which direction to push you in, can be a great advantage.

For this reason, many of my students come to me for help.

Stop spending long hours learning everything on your own!

I can help you achieve your language goals fast and with ease.

Does this sound like you?

  • Always shy about speaking with foreigners
  • Lack of fluency in the language - unable to keep a conversation going
  • Thinking in your native language - translating directly into the target language
  • A life-long beginner - always starting over every year to end up at the same place - at the beginning
  • Very basic level issues - problems with grammar, basic sentence structure, etc.
  • Struggling with languages in general

About Me & What I Do

My name's Kate Vanderpool and I’m a freelancer providing individual classes in English, French, and Czech for teenagers and adults of all language levels.

I create a unique communicative relationship with each of my students based on natural behavior, prompt reactions, and empathy.

Using creativity and humor to remember new things is a very important aspect of my relationship with all my students.

My students build a direct emotional connection with what they learn.

Moreover, I design my classes based on my Original Methodology.

To learn more about the teaching process and information about how I do what I do in detail, see FAQ.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you don't see an answer to your question,
you can send me an email from the Contact Me form.

I offer general and specialized classes, tutoring, graduation preparation, preparation for language and state exams and job interviews. (I also assist with content editing, proofreading, writing essays, and presentation preparation).

Most of my students are looking for a One-to-One, fully custom approach where the focus is uniquely on them and their goals and they can cast away their shyness.

They're motivated by the idea of being able to speak without fear, making less mistakes and being able to lead a conversation in a foreign language, whether that makes them the spokesperson for their family on vacation or empowers them to deal with foreign-language aspects of their job on a professional level.

They want to feel comfortable while communicating in their selected language.

Most students can see the progress really fast, generally. Especially where it matters most – spoken interaction.

They feel more confident thanks to their new skills and they want to use them!

They love that I'm always in a good mood, bringing loads of energy to each lesson, coming up with new, creative and effective activities, tools, and materials to fit what the student needs at the exact moment.

And I'm not very strict, but nothing escapes me:)

Based on your language level, needs and goals, we'll focus on a unique combination of the following language areas:

  1. Introduction to the language - always within the context of your native language
  2. Basic phrases for your purposes
  3. Genuine conversation giving context to your vocabulary extension
  4. A firm, but relaxed grasp of grammar rules
  5. Pronunciation and foreign accent reduction

One lesson takes 60 or 90 minutes. See pricing for more information.
